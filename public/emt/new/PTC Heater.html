Details
A self-regulating electric heater that automatically limits current above a certain temperature by utilizing the resistance characteristics of ceramic semiconductors. It is applied to Electric Vehicles (EV), Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEV), and HVAC systems to provide rapid heating, stable temperature maintenance, and improved energy efficiency Rated voltage: 13.5V ±0.1V Rated power: 1200 W +5% / −10% Operating voltage range: 8V to 16V Protection rating: IP50 Operating temperature: −30°C to +85°C Storage temperature: −40°C to +90°C Temperature rise: 13 to 15 K
