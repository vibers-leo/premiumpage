About us

Vision & Mission

We aim to become a global smart mobility partner by setting new standards for future industries through innovation and quality

VISION

A Global Top-Tier Partner by 2035 in Core Mobility Sensor and Control Solutions
EMT develops sensor and actuator technologies that enable safer driving. Our goal is to become the trusted partner customers turn to first by 2035 through integrated modules that combine sensors, MCUs, and software

MISSION

Quality You Trust, Technology for the Future
  • We prioritize customer trust and deliver the highest quality products and services
  • To achieve zero defects, we manage our processes using Statistical Process Control (SPC) techniques
  • We are creating a safe and sustainable corporate environment for everyone through our ESC initiatives

OUR GOALS
(2025 ~ 2030)

  • Reducing the defect rate
    Zero ppm
    We aim to achieve zero defects by 2030 through smart quality control and automated verification systems across all processes
  • Reduction of carbon emissions
