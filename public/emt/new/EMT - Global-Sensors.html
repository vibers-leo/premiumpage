Sensors

We enhance safety and efficiency through high-precision sensors and motion control solutions applied across fields such as autonomous driving, robotics, and intelligent transportation systems, supporting our customers’ technological competitiveness

    A self-regulating electric heater that automatically limits current above a certain temperature by utilizing the resistance characteristics of ceramic semiconductors.

    Limit Sensor
    A precision detection sensor that identifies the operating range of mechanical devices and outputs a switching signal when a preset limit position is reached.

    Resolver Sensor
    An analog angle detection sensor that converts mechanical angular displacement into electrical signals using electromagnetic induction.
