Sensors

We enhance safety and efficiency through high-precision sensors and motion control solutions applied across fields such as autonomous driving, robotics, and intelligent transportation systems, supporting our customers’ technological competitiveness
  • Details Inquiry
    TPMS Sensor
    A sensor that monitors tire pressure and temperature in real time and transmits the data to the driver.
  • Details Inquiry
    Electronic Accelerator Pedal Sensor (APS)
    A device that converts the pedal angle pressed by the driver into electrical signals and transmits them to the ECU to control vehicle speed.
  • Details Inquiry
    Speed Sensor
    A sensor that measures vehicle speed and transmits the data to the ECU and instrument cluster.
  • Details Inquiry
    Transfer Case Sensor
    A core component that switches between 2WD and 4WD based on driving conditions and detects and controls power distribution between the front and rear wheels.
  • Details Inquiry
    MAP Sensor
    A sensor that detects absolute pressure inside the intake manifold and transmits the data to the ECU.
  • Details Inquiry
    Steering Angle Sensor
    A sensor that measures the steering angle, direction, and rotational speed input by the driver and transmits the data to the ECU.
